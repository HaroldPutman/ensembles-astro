Join us at Ensembles for the first public screening of Todd Padgett's short film,
A Coffee Reunion. We'll watch the 8-minute film together, enjoy popcorn and drinks,
and have a chance to hear from and support this local filmmaker! 🍿 🎥

This free, family-friendly event is open to the entire community, and will be held
at Charlestown Ensembles (1120 Monroe Street).
