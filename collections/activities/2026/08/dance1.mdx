Dance 1 is a class for younger students that will explore multiple dance techniques, styles, and
performance skills. This session will focus on Musical Theater dance style.
Musical theater is a fun, high-energy, and expressive style that combines dance
technique with acting and storytelling. This dance style is great for beginners,
as it can incorporate many forms such as Jazz, Ballet, Tap, and more. This
class will learn about what musical theater dance is and the styles that go into
it. Classes will include warm-up exercises, technique, learning set
choreography, and a performance on the final day.

_This class is a safe place for ALL dancers, whether you are new or
experienced. It will be a lighthearted, energetic place to learn, enjoy, and
experience the world of dance with no judgment. ALL are welcome and
encouraged to join the beautiful world of dance!_
