Develop fundamentals of theater in this class focused on building strong audition skills.
Students will explore a variety of essential elements, including performing monologues and songs,
memorization techniques, dance calls, and improvisation. Whether you're preparing for your next
audition or simply want to grow as a performer, this class offers a supportive environment to
help you develop confidence and stage presence.
