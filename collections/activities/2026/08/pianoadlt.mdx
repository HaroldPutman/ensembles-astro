It's never too late to sit down at the piano. This welcoming class is for adults who have always
wanted to learn but never had the chance—or who tried years ago and want to start fresh. No experience
required. We'll build confidence from the very first lesson with clear, patient instruction in reading
music, technique, and playing songs you will enjoy. Small class size means plenty of
individual attention in a relaxed, supportive setting.
