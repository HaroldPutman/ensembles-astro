Come make a little mess, create sweet memories, and enjoy some special one-on-one time with your little artist!

During our Mommy & Me Toddler Art Time, we’ll begin by reading a fun children’s book together and then explore a hands-on art project inspired by the story.

This class is all about process art—giving little ones the freedom to explore colors, textures, and art materials while building important fine motor skills through painting, gluing, stamping, squishing, and creating!

There’s no “perfect” finished project here. 💜 Each child is encouraged to explore and create at their own pace with their grown-up right by their side.

Our goal is simple: creative play, growing little hands, and precious time spent making memories together.

- ✨ Perfect for toddlers and their grown-ups
- 🎨 Process-focused art activities
- 📚 Story time included
- 🖐️ Encourages fine motor skill development
- 💜 No art experience needed—messy hands are welcome
