Art is about learning the techniques, but it is also about learning how to utilize
those techniques to express yourself. In this class, we will explore many different
mediums of art such as: paint, sculpture, mixed media, and many more. This class is
open to all skill levels. I will teach techniques and give guidance on how to utilize a
material, but then the rest is up to the artist to explore and create their vision.
The goal is not an end result or product, it is about the skills learned through the
process of creation. We're not looking for perfection and previous knowledge, just the
drive to create and learn new skills.

_This class is a safe place for ALL artists, whether you are new or experienced. This
is a lighthearted, friendly, energetic place to learn, create, and explore the world of
art with no judgement. ALL are welcome and encouraged to join the creative world of
art._
