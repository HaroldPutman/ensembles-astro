Join us for the opening of our community art show "Once Upon a Time".
Enjoy light refreshments while you view the art and meet the artists
who created it.

"Once Upon a Time" is a collection of works from local artists paying tribute
to one of their favorite childhood books. This is a benefit for the
Charlestown-Clark County Public Library's Children's Department. 100% of art
sales go directly to the library.
