Join us for the community art show "Once Upon a Time". This is an interactive
experience where you will view art and have an opportunity to participate by
creating art of your own.

"Once Upon a Time" is a collection of works from local artists paying tribute
to some of their favorite childhood books. This is a benefit for the
Charlestown-Clark County Public Library's Children's Department. 100% of art
sales go directly to the library. Admission to this event is FREE.
