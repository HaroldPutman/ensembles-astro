Ahoy, mateys! Set sail for adventure at the Pirate Musical Theater Camp, hosted
by the City of Charlestown and Charlestown Ensembles for bold buccaneers in
3rd–8th grade. Landlubbers and seasoned performers alike are welcome to join
the crew!

This summer, our band of swabbies will be learning and performing _Pirates!
The Musical_, an action-packed tale following a salty crew featuring the likes
of Bluebeard, Yellowbeard, Whitebeard, and the notorious "No Beards." With the
Jolly Roger flying high, the plot thickens when a stowaway is discovered on
board. To solve the mystery, our pirates must seek the help of the legendary
King of the High "Cs." Join us for music, mayhem, and a rollicking good time
on the high seas!

This camp will meet at the [Arts & Enrichment Center](https://www.charlestownparks.com/arts-enrichment-center)
at 999 Water St. in Charlestown, but we'll kick it off with a special first session
at Ensembles 1120 Monroe St. on Sunday afternoon June 7, 2:00-8:00 PM.

The camp will culminate in two performances Friday June 12 at 2PM and 7 PM.

All campers will be in the musical, but if your "bilge rat" is interested in a
principal role, we will be holding OPTIONAL auditions on May 27 from 5-8 PM.
Reserve your audition time today at [https://charlestownensembles.com/pirate-auditions](/pirate-auditions).
