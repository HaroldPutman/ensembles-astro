This class will assist students in finding and improving singing voices. Whether you're
a novice or seeking additional coaching to help fine-tune your instrument, you'll be
given the tools to learn/improve vocal technique, build vocal strength, increase range,
and boost confidence. But most important of all, to have a ton of FUN playing and creating
with our voices!
