Elementary Homeschool Music is a fun, interactive class designed for children ages 5–11.
In this group setting, students will explore the fundamentals of music through singing,
rhythm games, movement activities, and hands-on experiences with a variety of classroom
instruments. The class will cover musical concepts such as beat, melody, tempo, and 
dynamics in a supportive and joyful environment. No prior experience is required—just
bring your curiosity and love for music! This course encourages creativity, active 
participation, and a lifelong appreciation of music while building listening skills, 
teamwork, and confidence.
