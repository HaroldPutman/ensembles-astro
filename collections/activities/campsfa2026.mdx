Summer Fun Art Camp is a five-day morning camp packed with colorful, hands-on projects inspired by all things summer. Campers will explore a variety of materials, techniques, and textures while creating multiple take-home art pieces each day.

This camp is designed to encourage creativity, build confidence, and give kids the freedom to experiment — without worrying about the mess at home. We embrace the paint, the splatter, the layering, and the creative process in a fun, supportive environment where every child can succeed.

Throughout the week, students will:
✨ Work with a variety of art mediums
✨ Learn foundational art techniques in an age-appropriate way
✨ Strengthen creative problem-solving skills
✨ Build friendships and lasting summer memories

No prior art experience is needed — just a willingness to create and have fun. All supplies are included.

It’s big color, creative exploration, and summer joy — minus the mess at your kitchen table.
