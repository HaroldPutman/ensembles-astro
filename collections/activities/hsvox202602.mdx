Homeschool Voice is a group class designed for homeschool students to develop their 
singing voices in a supportive and engaging environment. Students will learn vocal
technique, breathing, diction, and performance skills through fun exercises, group 
singing, and individual attention. The class features a variety of musical styles 
appropriate for ages 8–18 and encourages creativity, confidence, and collaboration.
No prior experience is required—just a willingness to sing and have fun!
