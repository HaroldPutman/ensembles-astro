Jessica Hartman is an artist and art educator based in Southern Indiana who is
passionate about making art accessible to everyone. She graduated with highest honors,
earning a 4.0 GPA while completing both a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Painting and a
Bachelor of Science in Art Education. Jessica enjoys working closely with her community,
creating opportunities for others to experience the joy, creativity, and connection that
art brings. This fall, she will begin graduate studies at the University of Louisville
while continuing to teach, create, and serve her community through the arts.
