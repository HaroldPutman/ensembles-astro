Ericka Johnson is the owner of
[Red Headed Princess Designs](https://www.whollyrustic.com/)
located in Sellersburg, IN. Ericka was born and raised here in Southern Indiana.
She and her husband have been married just four years but between them have
three great kids! They love Sellersburg and are happy to call it home! Ericka
loves painting and making things, and enjoys sharing that passion with others.

Ericka truly enjoys sharing her talents with young artists. She believes there
is something special about seeing a young person learn to love art. She says,
"Creating art is empowering and can help build confidence in kids and adults
alike."
