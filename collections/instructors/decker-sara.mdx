Sara Decker is a homeschool mom with over ten years experience, and a passion
for crafts and crocheting. She began crocheting at the age of twelve and has
developed her skills to an intermediate-to-expert level, creating blankets,
washcloths, scarves, and more. In recent years, she has specialized in
amigurumi, further expanding her expertise in the craft. Sara is also a proud
mother of five and grandmother of three.
