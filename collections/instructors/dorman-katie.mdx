Katie is a painter and illustrator who graduated from the Herron School of Art
and Design. Over the past three years, she has worked as a community art
teacher and enjoyed every step along the way. Katie is passionate about
encouraging students to explore their creativity and build confidence in all
they do. She believes that understanding oneself and one's passions is the
strongest foundation for creating anything. Katie continues to challenge and
inspire students to push themselves, try new approaches, and discover what
excites them most when making art.
