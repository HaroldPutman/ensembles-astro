Brad is a life-long Charlestown resident with over 35 years experience playing the
guitar. He has played with many local acts, including music of country, rock, blues,
and bluegrass. He is proficient in guitar, ukulele, and mandolin. Brad has also taught
people how to play the guitar through private lessons, local churches, and public
universities.
