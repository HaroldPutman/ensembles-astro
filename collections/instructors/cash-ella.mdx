Ella Cash is a graduate of Silver Creek High School and an enthusiastic member
of the Ensembles community. She plays trombone in the Ensembles Community Jazz
Band and directs the handbell choir, sharing her love of music with others. Ella
enjoys helping musicians of all ages experience the joy of making music together.
