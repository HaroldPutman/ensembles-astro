Sara is a Southern Indiana native and finds great joy in making music with others.
With her BA in Music and Masters in Elementary Education from U of L, she is
excited to teach the homeschool elementary music class. She is a happily married
mom of three, and can be found doing Jazzercise, baking bread, and learning new
crafts. Sara enjoys playing her french horn for her church and community.
