Kelsey Allen is excited to work with the vocal students at Ensembles! She has been
singing her whole life, but started performing when she was 13 years old. Kelsey has

students how to sing, but also in giving them opportunities that help them grow into
artists and performers themselves, able to use what she has taught them to chart
their own course in music for years to come. Kelsey is very goal-oriented in her
lessons and wants her students to set goals for themselves too. She finds this to be
