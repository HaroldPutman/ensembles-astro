Erin is currently the Assistant Director at the [Our Lady Theatre Department](https://olphna.org/student-life/theatre).
She has been an integral part of OLPH Theatre. She assisted in kick-starting their
program and co-directed, co-produced, and choreographed their debut show, Matilda
Jr. Since then, she has assisted the OLPH Theatre Director in The Little Mermaid Jr.
and Finding Nemo Kids.

Erin was previously the co-owner of
[Southern Indiana Elements of Art LLC](https://www.facebook.com/SoInElements/),
where she co-directed, co-produced, and assisted with choreography in Little Shop
of Horrors, SIX, and Mean Girls Jr., to name a few! Erin is an alumna of
[New Albany Theatre Arts](https://www.newalbanytheatrearts.org/) and
[Weber School of Dance](http://www.weberschoolofdance.com/), and she has a true
love for the arts. She has a passion for watching young artists develop
their confidence and find their voice through the journey of theatre!

In her spare time, you might find Erin watching a Halloween movie, hunting for
seashells at the beach, or hanging out with her fur babies! Her daughter is in
college in Hanover and is involved with their Theatre Department. Max, her
husband of almost 21 years, has created many of the staple set pieces you
see in her shows today.

Erin and her husband are happy to be part of the Ensembles team and can’t wait
to see what exciting things are in store!
