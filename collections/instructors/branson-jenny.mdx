Jenny Branson, MS, MT-BC, is an experienced music therapist, educator, and
performer with a passion for wellness, the potential of every person, and
collaboration for exceptional outcomes. She is a classically trained
percussionist and teaches percussion as well as music theory. Jenny works with
individuals, groups, facilities, and community organizations to provide music
therapy intervention, music lessons, arts and wellness resources, and community
music experiences that support connections and growth throughout the lifespan.
She lives in Southern Indiana, where she enjoys percussion performance with a
professional chamber ensemble, fundraising for animal rescue and arts
organizations, reading, travel, and time outdoors.
