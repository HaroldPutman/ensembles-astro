Rebecca grew up in Charlestown and has a master's degree in Music Education. Rebecca has
over 30 years of teaching experience both in the classroom and as a private teacher.
As a private piano teacher, Rebecca looks for creative ways to get her students making music
with others. She is super excited about Ensembles because she believes making music with others
is fun and helps us to develop our skills and grow musically.
